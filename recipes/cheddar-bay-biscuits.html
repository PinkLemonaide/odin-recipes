Cheaddar Bay Biscuits Recipe

Description

Get the delicious, taste of red lobster biscuits right at home. Before Red Lobster closes their doors for good learn how to enjoy them on your own.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of cheddar bay biscuit mix
  • 1 1/3 cups of water
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 3 oz of shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 a cup of melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon of dried parsley

Instructions

  1. Step 1


    Line your baking pan with parchment paper and preheat oven to 375 degrees F.

  2. Step 2


    Mix water, cheese and baking mix into a dough.

  3. Step 3


    Form dough into 6 balls and place on parchment paper. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until golden brown.

  4. Step 4


    Combine melted butter, garlic powder, salt, onion powder, and parsley in a small bowl.

  5. Step 5


    Brush butter mixutre over hot biscuits and serve.
