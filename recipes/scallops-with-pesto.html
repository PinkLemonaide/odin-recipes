Scallops with Pesto Cream Sauce

Description

A delicious, simple and affordable afternoon dish. Perfect for date nights or lazy nights indoors.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 1 pound of thawed Scallops
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Ground black pepper (to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 tablespoon of homemade pesto
  • 1 cup of heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tablespoon of capers

Steps

  1. Step 1


    Use salt and pepper to season thawed scallops.

  2. Step 2


    Over a medium heat, melt butter and slowly add scallops. Sear each side for 1 to 2 minutes, then mix well with pesto and capers.

  3. Step 3


    Add heavy cream and stir well before bringing to a boil.

  4. Step 4


    Remove from heat and serve.
